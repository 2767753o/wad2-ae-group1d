Help

Getting started

To get started, simply login or create an account by providing a username, email address, and a password.
Once you have created an account, you can get started by creating a post, or viewing other posts and commenting or liking them.
You can view your own profile and posts by clicking the profile button, as well as change your password, username, and email address.
You can search for posts or other users using the search bar too.

Creating a post

To create a post, click the Create Post button at the top of the page, and start typing.
You can also upload an image to go with your post by clicking the upload button.
Once you are finished creating your post, click the Create Post button and your post will be added to the feed.
Make sure to refresh the home page to see your post up there!

Interacting with other posts

You can like posts by clicking the heart icon, and comment on a post by opening the post, and typing your comment in the comment box.
You can view all the comments on a specific post by clicking on the post itself.
You can also view who has liked a post too.
By clicking on any profile, you can view that user's profile and posts.
