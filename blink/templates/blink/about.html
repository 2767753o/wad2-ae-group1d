About us

Welcome to Blink!

This is a social media platform without all the distractions and tempations of normal social media, so that you can express yourself and connect with others in a more meaningful way.

We pride ourselves on being a social media free from distractions, and want to introduce as many people as possible to this new form of social media.

Users on our platform can post only once a day, but are free to like and comment on posts as they please.

This feature hopes to enhance user experience by allowing users to focus on the content of the posts, rather than the quantity of them.

Quality over quantity!

So join us at Blink, the future of social media!
